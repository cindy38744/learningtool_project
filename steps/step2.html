#2 Stir, add the lid, and turn the temp down

Once you’ve added the rice, give it a quick stir and then place a lid on the pan. Immediately turn down the temperature (don’t wait to bring it back up to the boil again). For gas, cook at the very lowest temperature your hob can manage. For electric or induction, it may need to be a little higher. I use induction level 5 (of 9) because my lowest induction settings are extremely low heat.
